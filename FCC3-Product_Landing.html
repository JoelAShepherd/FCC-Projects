Who we are

We are one of the UK's biggest second-hand book stores. We have a vast range of books from all over the world - aged from nearly-new to over 150 years. We believe passionately that book if a book is worth reading then it is worth sharing.

What we do

We buy books second-hand from people from all walks of life and make them available for new readers. We sell the books instore but also dispatch book bundles - collections of books, either random or themed, which can be sent out in the post anywhere in the UK.
